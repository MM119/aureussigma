Minh Mai, CFA

Mr. Minh is a pioneer in applying factor investing strategies to Vietnamese financial markets using advanced artificial intelligence techniques. His research focuses on leveraging deep learning algorithms to identify alpha-generating factors specific to emerging Asian markets. At Fujiwara Capital, he developed proprietary neural network models that outperformed traditional statistical approaches for a $600M hedge fund portfolio. His innovative approach combines alternative data sources with machine learning to create Vietnam-focused investment frameworks that capture market inefficiencies.
